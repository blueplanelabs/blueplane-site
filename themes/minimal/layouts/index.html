Our Mission

Blue Plane is a non-profit research group funded by OSOCO for long-term thinking and working about software challenges.

Our mission is to incubate R&D projects aiming to a paradigm shift to redefine the software development experience and contributing to the human intellect augmentation in the spirit of Douglas Engelbart and Seymour Papert.

Our current approach to the problem is heavily influenced by the work of Douglas Engelbart (NLS), Ivan Sutherland (Sketchpad), Alan Kay (Dynabook and Smalltalk), Seymour Papert (Logo), Bret Victor (Dynamicland), the Pharo project, Tudor GÃ®rba (Glamorous Toolkit), and others.
